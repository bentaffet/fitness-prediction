Strava Fitness Predictor

Instructions:
  1. Click "Link Strava" to connect your account.
  2. After authorizing, your recent runs will be fetched automatically.
  3. Ensure Token Validated says "YES"
  4. Wait until you see "Fetched x runs"
  5. Be patient. If you wait more than 10 seconds it might be broken.
  6. Click "Compute Weekly Summary" to calculate modeled fitness for each week.
  7. Scroll to view the chart and weekly data.
